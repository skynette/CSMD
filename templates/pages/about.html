About Us

The Centre for Self Employment and Management Development (CSMD) Company is an indigenous business start up and management development trainer, consultant, vocational and entrepreneurship skills provider for all categories of employees and potential entrepreneurs including unemployed individuals in the society. She also caters for the needs of old workers (both the current and intending retirees).

Established in 2004, the organisation has trained well over 200 entrepreneurs including government officials in new business start ups, capacity building and entrepreneurial development. Additionally, similar number of business plans have been produced for participants that have resulted in the establishment of enduring enterprises in Edo State.

Purpose of the CSMD

The purpose of the CSMD is to create life opportunities for all; whether unemployed or employed individuals, and businesses whether small or big in the public and private sectors in Nigeria and beyond. This is achieved by providing quality business and vocational training and entrepreneurship development (for the acquisition of relevant skills for the identification of business opportunities and actualisation of the opportunities in the form of birth of a business). This also includes providing managerial and management capacity for effective performance in chosen business fields as well as training and services in management consulting, feasibility study/report writing, occupational transition, marketing research, information services and more.

Objectives of the Company

The main objectives of the CSMD are outlined as follows:

  • To harness the potentials of the unemployed towards positive engagement in the economic system for the benefit of all
  • To develop the capacity of the exclusive (financially and managerially neglected) small business managers/entrepreneurs for enhanced or improved performance
  • To as a conscious effort, provide a “combinational skills” to the young and other individuals who would form the future entrepreneurs;
  • To rejuvenate the older workers entrepreneurially to make them more meaningful for the well being of selves and for the economic uplift of society
  • To provide opportunities for the slow learners in formal educational institutions with a view to having a clearer understanding of themes (what is being taught) and most importantly
  • To enhance the capacity of the current workforce in organisations for improved performance.

The Mission

To deliver to our customers comparatively, quality services and training on business, entrepreneurship and vocational trade satisfactorily, within a short period, by highly talented resource persons, at affordable cost and profit to the organisation, while satisfying our various stake holders.

The Vision

To be the controller of training and development of the small and medium scale business owners as well as potential entrepreneurs in the informal sector in Nigeria and beyond.
